## What Are Smart Kitchen Appliances?

Smart kitchen appliances integrate technology to improve cooking and food preparation. They often connect with your smartphone via apps, allowing for remote control and programming to enhance convenience. For instance, I often use my smart oven to preheat while I'm at the grocery store, saving time when I return home.

### Key Features

- **Wi-Fi Connectivity:** Allow you to control appliances remotely.
- **Programmable Functions:** Enable you to set cooking times and temperatures for various recipes.
- **Compatibility with Smart Home Systems:** Integrate with other devices for streamlined operation.

Smart appliances make cooking simpler and more intuitive, relying on technology to serve up tasty meals and cut down on prep time.
