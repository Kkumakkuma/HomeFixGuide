## What Most Guides Get Wrong

When researching overhead garage storage solutions, you may come across various lists and reviews. Here are three contrarian insights that I think are often overlooked:

1. **Flexibility over Rigidity**: Many guides suggest choosing products with fixed shelves. In my view, adjustable options like the Rubbermaid FastTrack offer better long-term value. You can adapt them to your needs as your storage requirements change.

2. **Installation Ease**: I’ve often found that products requiring complex installation tend to deter users from maximizing their storage. Always look for systems that come with comprehensive installation guides or even customer service support.

3. **Weight Capacity Isn’t Everything**: While it's tempting to go for high-capacity options, consider how frequently you'll use those items. Sometimes, less robust but more accessible options, like the Atjot Folding Storage, can be a better daily choice.
