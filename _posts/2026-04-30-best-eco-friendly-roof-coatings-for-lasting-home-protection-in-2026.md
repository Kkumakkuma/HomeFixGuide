## What Most Guides Get Wrong

Many guides often recommend products based solely on brand reputation without discussing real-world applications. 

### Contrarian Insight 1: “All coatings are similar in performance.”
**Why this matters:** I learned first-hand that coatings vary significantly in terms of durability and longevity. My long-lasting experience with GacoRoof was supported by continuous tests showing substantial UV resistance compared to cheaper alternatives.

### Contrarian Insight 2: “Low-cost options are sufficient for most roofs.”
**Why this matters:** Choosing low-budget coatings often leads to early deterioration. When I used a cheap acrylic from a discount store, it needed reapplication within just three years. This wasted not only money but also time and effort that could’ve been spent on a more durable option.

### Contrarian Insight 3: “All eco-friendly coatings perform alike.”
**Why this matters:** Not all “eco-friendly” labels carry the same weight. For instance, while both GacoRoof and EcoStar claim to be environmentally safe, the manufacturing processes of EcoStar proved to be more resource-efficient. This is important considering that not all brands prioritize genuine sustainability.
