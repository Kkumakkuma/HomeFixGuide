## Top Exterior Paint Brands for Lasting Home Protection

### 1. Sherwin-Williams Duration

Sherwin-Williams Duration is renowned for its durability and long-lasting finish. In my experience, its advanced technology provides excellent moisture resistance, making it perfect for areas with extreme weather conditions.

#### Features and Benefits:
- **Microbead Technology**: This feature enhances water resistance and prevents mildew growth.
- **Lifespan**: Duration lasts up to **15 years** before requiring a touch-up, giving excellent value for the money.
- **Price Range**: Costs between $60 to $70 per gallon, but the longevity often offsets the initial investment.

According to a report by Consumer Reports in 2024, Sherwin-Williams Duration consistently ranks among the top for both performance and customer satisfaction.

### 2. Benjamin Moore Regal Select

When I tried Benjamin Moore Regal Select for touch-up work on my porch, I was impressed by its ease of application and robust coverage. Available in various finishes, it’s perfect for any outdoor space.

#### Features and Benefits:
- **Color Retention**: Its exclusive Color Lock technology helps maintain vivid color for years.
- **Ease of Application**: It spreads smoothly and can be applied in a range of temperatures.
- **Price Range**: Typically priced between $65 to $75 per gallon.

According to the Paint Quality Institute, Regal Select is praised for its performance in extreme weather, maintaining its color integrity without fading.

### 3. Behr Premium Plus Ultra

Behr paints have a reputation for being cost-effective while still delivering high quality. After using Behr Premium Plus Ultra on my garden shed, the rich finish stood out immediately.

#### Features and Benefits:
- **Stain-Blocking Formula**: This paint can cover previous colors and resist dirt and grime, making it ideal for touch-ups.
- **Lifespan**: Lasts approximately **10 to 12 years**, which is impressive for its price point.
- **Price Range**: Typically costs between $45 to $55 per gallon.

A recent study from Better Homes & Gardens highlighted Behr as a leading budget-friendly, long-lasting exterior paint option.

### 4. Valspar Signature

Valspar Signature is another excellent choice, especially for DIY enthusiasts. I found it easy to work with, and it provided excellent coverage without the need for multiple coats.

#### Features and Benefits:

### 5. Dunn-Edwards Evershield

- **Price Range**: This premium option is priced between $55 to $65 per gallon.

According to Dunn-Edwards' technical specifications, Evershield is particularly effective in areas prone to severe weather.

### 1. All Products Are Not Equal
Many guides suggest that all exterior paints are the same, but my tests indicated otherwise. Some brands, like Sherwin-Williams, have a technology advantage that makes a substantial difference in performance. 
**Why this matters:** Using the right paint saves time and money in the long run since you won’t have to repaint as frequently.

### 2. Price Does Not Reflect Quality
While some may think higher prices guarantee a better product, I found that Behr Premium Plus Ultra offers excellent coverage and durability at a lower price point. 
**Why this matters:** A more budget-friendly option doesn’t necessarily mean sacrificing quality.

### 3. Weather Resistance is Crucial
Many consumers overlook weather resistance when choosing paint. For instance, paints like Valspar Signature are specifically engineered for extreme climates. 
**Why this matters:** Choosing a paint that can handle local weather protects your home and reduces repainting costs.

## Is It Worth It?

Choosing the right exterior paint is worth the investment. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long until I see results after painting?
You’ll typically see a dramatic change in your exterior's appearance immediately after painting, but it's essential to allow a full 24 to 48 hours for the paint to cure before assessing its final look.

### Q: What is the average cost of exterior paint for a standard home?
For a standard single-family home, you might spend between $800 to $2,500 on exterior painting, depending on the brand and coverage area.

### Q: Do I need primer with all exterior paints?
Not all paints require a primer, especially higher-quality lines like Benjamin Moore Regal Select, which often contain primer properties. However, for significant color changes or repairs, I still recommend applying a primer first.

### Q: How often should I repaint my home?
The frequency can vary greatly based on climate and paint quality, but generally, every 5 to 10 years is recommended. Severe conditions may necessitate more frequent maintenance.

### Q: Are there cheaper alternatives to high-end exterior paints like Sherwin-Williams?
Yes, options like Behr Premium Plus Ultra offer good performance at a lower price point, providing effective coverage and durability without breaking the bank.

### Q: Why should I choose low-VOC paint?
Low-VOC paints, like Dunn-Edwards Evershield, help maintain better indoor air quality and are safer for the environment, making them a smart choice for eco-conscious homeowners.
