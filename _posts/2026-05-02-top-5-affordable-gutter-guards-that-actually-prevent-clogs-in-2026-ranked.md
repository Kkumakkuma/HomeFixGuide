## What Are Gutter Guards?

Gutter guards are protective covers that you install over your gutters to prevent leaves, twigs, and debris from clogging the gutter system. If you're tired of cleaning out gutters manually, a quality guard can save you the backache and prevent water damage to your home by ensuring efficient drainage.

### Types of Gutter Guards

1. **Screen Gutter Guards:** These are typically made from metal or plastic and cover the entire gutter system. They allow water to flow through while keeping out larger debris.
  
2. **Mesh Gutter Guards:** Similar to screens but finer, mesh guards can catch smaller particles. They are usually made from stainless steel or aluminum.

3. **Reverse Curve Gutter Guards:** These direct rainwater around a curve and into the gutter while allowing leaves to fall to the ground. 

4. **Bottle Brush Gutter Guards:** These feature bristles standing upright within the gutter. They allow water to flow while blocking debris but can accumulate plant matter over time.
