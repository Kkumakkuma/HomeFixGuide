## The Importance of Rodent Exclusion  
Rodents can cause significant damage if not adequately managed. According to the National Pest Management Association, rodents are responsible for over $1 billion in damages annually in the U.S. alone. You’ll find gnawed wires, insulation problems, and even health risks due to droppings and urine. Prompt action with the right exclusion service can save you from exorbitant repair bills and reduce health hazards from exposure to rodent-borne diseases.
