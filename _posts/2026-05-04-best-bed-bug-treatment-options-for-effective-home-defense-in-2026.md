## Understanding Bed Bugs

### What Are Bed Bugs?

Bed bugs are microscopic pests that thrive on human blood. They are often brought into a home through second-hand furniture, luggage, or clothing. Adult bed bugs can survive up to a year without feeding, making them difficult to eradicate. According to the CDC, bed bug bites can cause discomfort and have psychological effects, leading to sleepless nights and anxiety.

### Signs of Infestation

If you wake up with unexplained bites or find small blood stains on your sheets, you might have a bed bug problem. Look for:

-  Dark spots on your mattress—these are bed bug excrement.
-  Shed skins that they leave behind when they molt.
-  Live bugs hiding in crevices during the day.

### Why Professional Help Might Not Always Be Necessary

Although hiring pest control can be effective, it can also be costly, often ranging from $300 to $1000. If you prefer tackling the problem yourself, there are several excellent DIY treatments worth considering.
