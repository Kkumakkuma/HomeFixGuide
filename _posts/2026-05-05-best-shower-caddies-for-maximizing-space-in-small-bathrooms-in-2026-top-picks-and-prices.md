### Best Practices for Maintenance and Longevity

Finding the best caddy is just the beginning; proper maintenance is essential to keep it looking new and functional. After purchasing the Simplehuman Adjustable Shower Caddy, I learned a few tricks to prolong its lifespan:

#### Regular Cleaning Routine

I set a monthly reminder to clean my shower caddy. Just using mild soap and a soft sponge has worked wonders. 

- **Materials**: For stainless steel and chrome finishes, I avoid abrasive cleaners that can scratch the surface. Instead, I use a mixture of vinegar and water for a natural shine. 

#### Effective Water Drainage Orientation

One issue I initially faced with the Zenna caddy was pooling water at the bottom. After some trial and error, I reoriented the caddy slightly to allow for better drainage, dramatically reducing the buildup of soap scum and mildew.

**Lesson Learned**: Make sure your caddy’s design allows for proper drainage in the shower area. I was shocked by how much of a difference this made in maintenance.
