### Claim 2: “You must buy the most expensive option for quality.”

Many suggest splurging on premium brands for a fulfilling experience. 

**Why this matters:** In my experience, the Simple Houseware Letter Organizer outperformed my expectations at a third of the cost of some high-end brands. I saved $45 while receiving quality functionality.

### Claim 3: “Color doesn’t matter in organizational tools.”

It’s easy to assume that functionality is all that counts, but aesthetics play a significant role. 

**Why this matters:** A design that resonates with you makes the workspace more inviting. When I transitioned to the Poppin organizer, I found myself more motivated to maintain organization because it was visually satisfying.
