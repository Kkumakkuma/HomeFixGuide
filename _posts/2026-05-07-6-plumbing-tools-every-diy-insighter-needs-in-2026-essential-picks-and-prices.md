## 1. Pipe Wrench: An Essential for Any DIY Plumber

The pipe wrench is a cornerstone to any plumbing toolkit. Unlike other types of wrenches, the tooth design helps grip and rotate pipes, which makes it invaluable during both repairs and installations.

### Key Features of Pipe Wrenches

- **Material:** Most pipe wrenches are made from durable steel, offering longevity and strength.
- **Size Variations:** They come in various sizes; the most common ones are 14, 18, and 24 inches.
- **Adjustability:** A good pipe wrench should be easily adjustable to fit different pipe sizes without slipping.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **Ridgid Heavy-Duty Pipe Wrench:**  This wrench is about $30-$40 and is praised for its excellent grip.
- **DeWalt DWHT70284 10" Pipe Wrench:** Retailing around $20, it offers versatility for smaller spaces.
- **Husky 18-Inch Pipe Wrench:** About $25, known for its durable construction and easy handling.

With a sturdy pipe wrench, I've been able to tackle projects from installing new faucets to repairing old pipes. 

## 2. Plumber's Snake: Clearing Clogs with Ease

When faced with clogged drains, a plumber's snake (also known as a drain auger) is a must-have tool. It helps in breaking through stubborn blockages without needing to call in professionals.

### Key Features of Plumber's Snakes

- **Action:** The snake’s coiled steel cable can flexibly maneuver through pipes.
- **Length and Diameter:** Snakes vary in length (usually between 25-75 feet) and diameter to accommodate different pipe sizes.
- **Flexibility in Use:** Some models have powered options that require minimal effort while providing maximum reach.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **RIDGID 59787 K-3 Toilet Auger:** Priced around $25, this tool specializes in toilet clogs.
- **Vermillion 50 FT Drain Snake:** Available for about $20, offering good flexibility for household clogs.
- **DEWALT 20V MAX Drain Auger:** Costs around $110, this powered model excels in eliminating tough blockages effortlessly.

During one of my DIY plumbing tasks, using a plumber's snake saved me from having to pay a $150 service fee to a plumbing service. 

## 3. Adjustable Wrench: Versatility in Small Spaces

This handy tool comes in handy for a multitude of repairs. It allows you to change sizes, making it adaptable for various tasks, especially in tight spaces.

### Key Features of Adjustable Wrenches

- **Jaw Size:** They usually range from 6 to 12 inches in length.
- **Easy Adjustability:** One smooth motion allows for quick adjustments.
- **Grip Types:** Look for rubberized grips for comfort during usage.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **Kobalt 6-inch Adjustable Wrench:** Around $16, it provides great value for small jobs.
- **Craftsman Adjustable Wrench 12-Inch:** Priced at approximately $20, it’s sturdy for larger nuts and bolts.
- **Milwaukee 48-22-7500 10” Adjustable Wrench:** Costs about $30 and is known for both strength and portability.

I've found my adjustable wrench useful for everything, but especially while reconnecting supply lines under my kitchen sink.

## 4. Plumber's Torch: Soldering Like a Pro

A plumber's torch is essential for anyone who plans on doing more than just basic plumbing. It’s specifically designed for soldering copper pipes, enabling professional-grade results.

### Key Features of Plumber's Torches

- **Temperature Control:** Look for models with adjustable flames for versatility.
- **Size:** Compact models are suitable for tighter areas.
- **Fuel Type:** Many torches are powered by propane, but some models use MAPP gas for a hotter flame.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **Bernzomatic TS8000 Trigger Start Torch:** Costs around $45; it is favored for its ease of use and reliability.
- **Blazer GT8000 Pro Torch:** About $35, known for its compact nature and high-temperature levels.
- **Primus Classic Trail Stove:** A safe option for around $50, suitable for larger jobs.

I once had my neighbor recommend a plumber's torch to solder pipes while I installed radiant heating. It was worth every penny because it made the job easier and the results cleaner.

## 5. Toilet Auger: Your Best Friend for Clogs

When toilets clog, panic often ensues. That’s where a specialized toilet auger comes into play—large enough to tackle tough clogs without damaging the porcelain.

### Key Features of Toilet Augers

- **Length:** Generally, they are about 3 to 6 feet long.
- **Design:** Flexible yet sturdy, designed specifically for toilet drains.
- **Protective Sleeve:** This feature prevents damage to toilet surfaces.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **Ridgid 59787 K-3 Toilet Auger:** About $35, and it’s specifically designed to clear toilet clogs.
- **DrainRooter Toilet Auger:** Priced at $25; it’s lightweight but effective.
- **Vermillion Toilet Auger:** Costs around $30 and is known for its user-friendly design.

I had a memorable experience in February where my toilet clogged unexpectedly. Thankfully, the toilet auger was readily available, allowing me to avoid a hefty bill for professional plumbing.

## 6. Basin Wrench: Tight Spots, No Problem

A basin wrench is designed for working in tricky areas, such as under sinks, where your hands can’t easily fit. 

### Key Features of Basin Wrenches

- **Length and Reach:** The tool is often adjustable to reach difficult spots.
- **Pivoting Head:** Ideal for tightening and loosening nuts that are otherwise inaccessible.
- **Durability:** Most are made from strong steel for longevity.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **Ridgid 14058 10-Inch Basin Wrench:** Costs around $19, robust yet easy to handle.
- **DeWalt DWMT75289 1/2-Inch Basin Wrench:** Priced at approximately $22.
- **Kobalt 7-Inch Basin Wrench:** About $17 and effective for easy household tasks.

Earlier this month, I needed to change my faucet, but the space under the sink was minimal. My basin wrench was essential to make that job a breeze.

## 9. Water Pressure Gauge: Monitoring for Efficiency

Understanding your home’s water pressure is crucial for avoiding plumbing issues. A water pressure gauge allows you to monitor the system's pressure, helping you pinpoint issues before they escalate.

### Key Features of Water Pressure Gauges

- **Pressure Range:** Typically measures from 0 to 300 PSI, accommodating most residential needs.
- **Dial Size:** A larger dial can make it easier to read the pressure at a glance.
- **Compatibility:** Many come with adapters to fit various types of spigots and hoses.

### Recommended Brands and Pricing

- **DuraWater Water Pressure Gauge:** Costs around $25; it's praised for its straightforward setup and clear readings.
- **Primefit Pressure Gauge:** Available for about $15, known for its durable design and accuracy.
- **Gardena Water Pressure Gauge:** Priced at approximately $18, offers versatility for garden hoses and irrigation systems.

When I first bought my house, I noticed several appliances struggling under low water pressure. After purchasing the DuraWater Gauge and discovering my pressure was far below the recommended levels, I made adjustments to the system that significantly improved water flow throughout my home. It was a small investment that made a remarkable difference.
