Built-in features like pull-out trays, lazy Susans, and pull-down racks can change the game when it comes to maximizing functionality. ## What's the Difference Between Flat-Pack Cabinetry and Custom Cabinets?

When choosing kitchen cabinets for small spaces, you’ll often find two main categories: flat-pack and custom cabinetry. 

### Flat-Pack Cabinetry

Flat-pack cabinets, such as those from IKEA or Wayfair, are pre-manufactured and shipped in pieces that you assemble at home. In my experience, I saved a significant amount of money—up to 50% less compared to custom solutions—due to the cost-effective nature of flat-pack designs.

### Custom Cabinets

While custom cabinets are tailored to your specific needs, they come with much higher price tags, averaging from $200 to $1,200 per linear foot. These cabinets can fit non-standard spaces beautifully. However, I realized that choosing custom could extend lead times, sometimes up to 16 weeks, while flat-pack options typically arrive in 1-2 weeks.

**Why this matters:** If you're on a tight deadline or budget, flat-pack may be the best choice for immediate needs.

## How Much Do Kitchen Cabinets Cost in 2026?

When it comes to the cost of kitchen cabinets in 2026, the prices can vary significantly based on several factors:

1. **Material:** Solid wood cabinets can range from $200 to $1,200 per linear foot, while laminate cabinets max out around $200.
2. **Style and Finish:** Painted or stained finishes often increase costs by 20-50%.
3. **Size of Kitchen:** Larger kitchens naturally require more cabinetry, potentially increasing overall costs.
4. **Brand:** Choices from well-known brands such as KraftMaid or Schuler typically come at a premium.

For instance, I paid around $800 for my IKEA Sektion set, which beautified my kitchen without denting my wallet, while a comparable custom option would have likely cost upwards of $2,400.
